What is Dead By Daylight Hub all about?

News & Infos

Dead By Daylight Hub will keep you updated with all the latest news surrounding Dead By Daylight. This includes new chapters, patch notes, major and minor changes to the game and its mechanics, new content drops etc.

Submit Your Ideas

One big benefit you will get from DBD Hub is option for submiting your own killer/survivor ideas. We will review them and publish them in "Idea's" section. As well, one of the best one's will be forwarded to developers!

Latest Content Drops

Give your favorite survivor or killer new comsetic items. We will make sure to let you know when new content-cosmetic related drop is available! You can buy these items in in-game store section.

Content Creators

If you are a content creator we will be giving you additional support by promoting your channel and work! In our "Content Creators" section, you will be able to find latest live streams related to Dead By Daylight only.
